Welcome to Allegria Restaurant!

We offer international cuisine dishes, most of them, prepared on the spot, with fresh ingredients.

The Restaurant is open Monday to Friday between 18:00-22:00, Saturday and Sunday is closed, except for the events.

The Restaurant is also closed on national holidays and during employee breaks (01.08.2016-15.08.2016, 22.12.2016-30.12.2016, ...).

Smoking is forbidden inside buildings and is punishable by law (Law 15/2016).

à la carte Menu

Allegria Restaurant offers diverse international dishes served in a pleasant athmosphere by lovely waitresses. This might be the ideal spot the share with family and friends.

Restaurant Rooms

We offer 3 rooms: for 20, 50 and 100 people. The usual activity is happening in our main room, Allegria, with a capacity of 50 people. The other rooms are opened depending on the scheduled events.
